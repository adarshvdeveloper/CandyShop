Candy Shop

Originally candy was not made with sugar but pure honey. During the middle ages, these candies were exclusively prepared for cooling and soothing sore throat. Another invention was the candies blended with spices like ginger, cloves, almonds, aniseed etc. dipped in honey syrup or sugar coating for healing digestive problems.
